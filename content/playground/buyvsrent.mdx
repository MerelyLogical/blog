## Formulae

Renting:

$$
C_{t+1}=(C_t + I - R)\cdot(1+r_{mn})
$$

$$
r_{mn}=(1+r_{yr})^{\frac{1}{12}}-1
$$

Buying:

$$
M_0=H-D
$$

$$
X_0=C_0-D-B
$$

$$
N=12Y
$$

$$
P=\frac{M_0\cdot m_{mn}(1+m_{mn})^N}{(1+m_{mn})^N-1}
$$

If $m_{mn}=0$, then $P=\frac{M_0}{N}$.

$$
M_{t+1}=M_t\cdot(1+m_{mn})-P_t
$$

$$
X_{t+1}=(X_t+I-P_t)\cdot(1+r_{mn})
$$

Here $P_t=P$ for most mortgage months, the final payment clears remaining balance, and $P_t=0$ after payoff.

$$
NW_t=H-M_t+X_t
$$

$$
m_{mn}=(1+m_{yr})^{\frac{1}{12}}-1
$$

Legend: $C$ cash, $C_0$ starting cash, $I$ monthly income, $R$ monthly rent, $D$ deposit, $B$ one-time buying cost, $H$ home value, $M$ mortgage balance, $X$ invested cash, $Y$ mortgage term in years, $N$ mortgage term in months, $P$ scheduled monthly mortgage payment, $P_t$ actual payment in month $t$.
